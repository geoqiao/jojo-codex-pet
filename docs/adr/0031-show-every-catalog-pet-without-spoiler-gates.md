# Show every catalog pet without spoiler gates

The Gallery will display every named Character Pet, Stand Pet, evolved Stand Form, and released artwork directly. It will not place antagonist identities, ability modes, or released artwork behind spoiler covers, `<details>` gates, or reveal buttons. Planned pets remain visible as named Planned entries until their real artwork and packages pass the release gate. This supersedes only the spoiler-reveal portion of ADR 0018; the Astro architecture, catalog filtering, language selection, and other focused interactions remain unchanged.
