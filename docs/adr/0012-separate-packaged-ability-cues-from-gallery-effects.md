# Separate packaged ability cues from gallery effects

Every Codex Pet package will visibly express its defining power through a pixelated Core Ability Cue that remains opaque, attached to or overlapping the subject, and contained inside the sprite cell. More expansive aura, particle, and impact treatments belong to a separate Showcase Effect rendered by the gallery, preserving an ability-forward presentation without baking noncompliant detached effects into the installable v2 atlas.
