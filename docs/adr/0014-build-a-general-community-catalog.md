# Build a general community catalog with a JoJo flagship

The public product will be a general Community Catalog for project-maintained and externally sourced Codex Pets, with the JoJo Fan Collection as its first Flagship Collection rather than the boundary of the whole site. This supports community-scale browsing, collections, creators, and future submissions while keeping JoJo-specific IP, roster, visual-system, and release decisions scoped to that collection; it also introduces broader provenance, licensing, moderation, and takedown responsibilities for the catalog.
