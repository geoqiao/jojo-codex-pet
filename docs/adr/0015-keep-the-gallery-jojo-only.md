# Keep the public gallery focused on JoJo

The general Community Catalog direction in ADR-0014 is superseded: the repository and website will remain a vertical JoJo Fan Project and will not list unrelated external pets. This preserves a coherent identity and keeps browsing, rights notices, moderation, and collection structure tailored to JoJo, accepting a smaller catalog; whether approved contributors may submit additional JoJo pets remains a separate decision.
