# Wave 2 Part 5 Visual Brief

Status: production brief for the second Wave 2 batch. These are project-authored free-color interpretations, not canonical JoJo palettes. Every pet remains unpublished until the complete Part 5 batch passes engineering QA and owner review.

## Shared contract

- Unified chibi pixel system: compact three-head-tall full body, chunky pixel clusters, hard dark contours, limited palette, and a readable `192x208` silhouette.
- One locked signature palette per pet. Character, Stand, and lineage forms coordinate through selected accents without using identical palettes.
- State meanings remain fixed. Character-specific performance and ability cues may vary only when they reinforce the assigned Codex state.
- Ability cues must be opaque, hard-edged, and attached to or overlapping the pet. No detached creatures, plants, arrows, clocks, screens, shards, symbols, shadows, scenery, or combined character-and-Stand pets.
- Gold Experience and Gold Experience Requiem are separate lineage pets. Neither form transforms into the other inside an animation row.

## Giorno Giovanna

Identity lock: compact poised teenager, three large forehead rolls joined cleanly to the hair mass, long rear braid, high open chest panel, simplified ladybug-like ornaments, tailored suit, slim trousers, and controlled hand poses.

Signature palette: midnight teal `#16263a`, rich orchid `#9257c7`, warm apricot `#ef8f5b`, bright mint `#59cba9`, pale gold `#e4bf58`, and warm ivory `#f4e8cf`.

Core ability cue: a small opaque mint-and-gold life-growth seam physically fused to a fingertip, palm, sleeve, or touched body edge. It may suggest sprouting or restoration through attached pixel facets in compatible task, recovery, and review states. Gold Experience never appears inside this pet.

## Gold Experience

Identity lock: compact elegant armored humanoid Stand, smooth beetle-like helmet, narrow face, rounded shoulder armor, segmented forearms, readable chest and joint ornaments, and a balanced athletic stance.

Signature palette: deep indigo `#21183b`, honey gold `#d6a942`, bright jade `#43c39f`, hot coral `#ef6b70`, warm cream `#f3e6c5`, and cool sky `#62bdd0`.

Pair relationship: shares Giorno's mint, gold, and warm coral family while shifting the dominant silhouette to indigo armor and honey plates.

Core ability cue: an opaque attached jade-and-coral life facet that grows from or rejoins a fist, forearm, or touched armor edge. No detached animals, plants, floating leaves, or loose fragments.

## Gold Experience Requiem

Identity lock: evolved compact armored humanoid Stand, taller crown-like helmet crest, calm mask, broader collar and shoulder geometry, layered torso plates, segmented limbs, and a more sovereign upright silhouette than Gold Experience.

Signature palette: charcoal violet `#19172b`, pale ivory `#efe8d0`, solar gold `#dfb64a`, royal amethyst `#7655b8`, clear turquoise `#43bdb0`, and raspberry `#ca507d`.

Lineage relationship: retains Gold Experience's gold and life-turquoise accents while using ivory crown armor, amethyst structure, and a distinct higher-status silhouette.

Core ability cue: a compact opaque turquoise-and-gold return pulse integrated into the palm, chest plate, or forearm. It may contract back toward its origin across active-work, failed-reset, or review states. No detached arrow, ring, clock, numeral, or environment-wide effect.

## Diavolo

Identity lock: compact imposing adult, long layered spotted hair with a clean readable silhouette, sharp brows, simplified mesh-like chest panel rendered as a few large diamonds, fitted arm bands, patterned trousers, and tense guarded hand poses.

Signature palette: ink plum `#181426`, pale aqua `#b9e2dc`, deep fuchsia `#cf4d91`, burnt apricot `#df764d`, antique gold `#d6ad49`, and ice blue `#bfe7ed`.

Core ability cue: an opaque fuchsia-and-aqua time notch fused to an eye, palm, wrist, or torso edge. It may skip, compress, or close as an attached two-layer pixel accent in tense waiting, active work, failure, and review. King Crimson never appears inside this pet.

## King Crimson

Identity lock: powerful compact armored humanoid Stand, crown-like forehead plate, mask-like face with a clearly integrated secondary forehead face cue, broad shoulder armor, heavy forearms, layered abdominal plates, and large readable diamond or grid motifs.

Signature palette: dark navy `#15182a`, brick rose `#bd4e58`, petrol teal `#2f9893`, pale cream `#efe5ca`, warm gold `#d5ad4c`, and cool lavender `#9c83c3`.

Pair relationship: shares Diavolo's rose, aqua-teal, and antique-gold accents while moving the dominant body colors to dark navy and brick armor.

Core ability cue: an opaque attached teal-and-gold temporal slice integrated into a fist, forehead plate, or torso seam. Epitaph remains a restrained forehead-face cue and attached prediction pulse, never a floating face, clock, screen, or separate pet.

## Batch acceptance

All five pets require complete 1536x2288 Codex Pet v2 atlases, three isolated blind direction reviews, explicit labeled direction semantics, continuity review, independent final visual QA, package validation, and one Part 5 QA overview highlighting every accepted warning before release.
