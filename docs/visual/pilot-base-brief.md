# Pilot Base Design Brief

Approval status: all four base designs were approved on 2026-07-19; their complete V2 packages were publicly released on 2026-07-21.

These four images establish the visual source of truth for the Part 3 Pilot Set before any animation row is generated. They are original unofficial fan-art interpretations, not traced panels, anime frames, game sprites, or attempts to reproduce a living artist's exact drawing style.

## Shared system

- JoJo-themed chibi pixel pet with an approximately 2.5-head proportion.
- One centered full-body subject in a neutral three-quarter-front pose.
- Bold near-black contour, deliberate low-resolution pixel clusters, crisp stair-stepped edges, and limited flat colors.
- Fashion-editorial complementary color blocking with dramatic but project-authored colors.
- Silhouette and signature costume anatomy must remain recognizable at small Codex-pet size.
- No text, logos, watermark, scenery, floor, cast shadow, glow, detached effect, or second character.
- Flat `#00ff00` chroma background; that color must not appear in the subject.

## Hero pair

### Jotaro Kujo

- ID: `part-03-jotaro-kujo`
- Identity anchors: fused cap-and-hair silhouette, long school coat, broad collar, short shoulder chain, stern brows, wide-legged stance.
- Signature palette: midnight ink `#171a33`, seafoam `#7ed6c4`, coral `#ff6b6b`, antique gold `#e8c66a`, warm skin, near-black contour.
- Read: compact, immovable, guarded confidence.

### Star Platinum

- ID: `part-03-star-platinum`
- Identity anchors: powerful humanoid build, swept long hair, circlet, large shoulder pieces, gloves and boots, intense eyes.
- Signature palette: royal plum `#5a2c6f`, electric cyan `#42c7d9`, shared coral `#ff6b6b`, bone `#f4e6c2`, near-black contour.
- Read: explosive power held under precise control.

The pair shares coral as an accent while preserving clearly different dominant colors and silhouettes.

## Antagonist pair

### DIO

- ID: `part-03-dio`
- Identity anchors: high swept hair, heart headband ornament, cropped structured jacket, fitted trousers, heart-shaped knee details, theatrical upright posture.
- Signature palette: oxblood `#7a1f3d`, ivory `#f2e7cf`, acid cyan `#36c9c6`, molten gold `#d9a441`, near-black contour.
- Read: glamorous control, predatory calm, absolute self-belief.

### The World

- ID: `part-03-the-world`
- Identity anchors: massive armored humanoid, angular helmet and face guard, broad shoulder armor, compact waist armor, heavy gloves and boots, clockwork-like structural motifs without text or numerals.
- Signature palette: deep turquoise `#1a686d`, shared molten gold `#d9a441`, shared oxblood `#7a1f3d`, ivory `#f2e7cf`, near-black contour.
- Read: compressed force and frozen-time menace.

The pair shares oxblood and molten gold accents, but DIO is fabric-forward and The World is armor-forward.

Approve or request changes to silhouette, proportion, face, costume anchors, palette, and pair coordination. Animation poses, ability cues, motion timing, and v2 look directions begin only after all four base designs are approved.
