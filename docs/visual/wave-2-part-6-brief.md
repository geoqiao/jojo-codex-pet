# Wave 2 Part 6 Visual Brief

Status: production brief for the first Part 6 batch. These are project-authored free-color interpretations, not canonical JoJo palettes. All six pets passed engineering QA and owner review and were publicly released on 2026-07-21.

## Shared contract

- Unified chibi pixel system: compact three-head-tall full body, chunky pixel clusters, hard dark contours, limited palette, and a readable `192x208` silhouette.
- One locked signature palette per pet. Character, Stand, and lineage forms coordinate through selected accents without using identical palettes.
- State meanings remain fixed. Character-specific performance and ability cues may vary only when they reinforce the assigned Codex state.
- Ability cues must be opaque, hard-edged, and attached to or overlapping the pet. No detached artifacts, symbols, scenery, text, floating projectiles, or combined character-and-Stand pets.
- Enrico Pucci and lineage Stands remain separate pets. White Snake, C-MOON, and Made in Heaven are distinct standalone forms and should not be visually merged.

## Jolyne Cujoh

Identity lock: compact athletic young woman, high ponytail silhouette, layered jacket-and-shirt proportions, bold hair movement, fitted skirt/leggings geometry, controlled upright stance, and expressive hands.

Signature palette: deep indigo `#181c33`, storm blue `#2f6e8d`, mint `#4cb29f`, warm coral `#e58a3f`, cream `#f0e1c6`, and smoke gray `#b3b2d3`.

Core ability cue: an attached thread-like water- or cloth-like liberation ribbon that remains connected to a wrist, shoulder, or waist edge, emphasizing momentum or rescue-like release in active and recovery states.

## Stone Free

Identity lock: compact armored humanoid Stand, streamlined torso plates, angular shoulders, streamlined forearms, readable thread control gesture language, and strong vertical posture.

Signature palette: dark violet `#1c1730`, cobalt blue `#2e4a84`, electric cyan `#4fd3d2`, pale peach `#e9bf67`, silver ash `#dfe7ef`, and warm charcoal `#2b2b2f`.

Pair relationship: shares Jolyne's darker indigo family while using cleaner cyan and cobalt for a distinctly stand-like silhouette.

Core ability cue: a compact opaque, attached thread knot or filament bundle that appears at a hand, shoulder seam, or waist. The cue should look tangible, grounded, and never detached.

## Enrico Pucci

Identity lock: compact priest-like adult, shaved upper scalp with clean neckline, solemn expression, strict lapel geometry, straight formal silhouette, and restrained wrist articulation.

Signature palette: black-olive `#191a1f`, ash gray `#5d5f63`, dusty olive `#4e5e4f`, muted gold `#bda65d`, muted violet `#70608a`, and cream `#e4ddc8`.

Core ability cue: an attached restrained white-gold prayer-line arc fused to a hand or chest seam, used as a focused correction or judgment cue in active, failed, and review states.

## Whitesnake

Identity lock: compact insectoid Stand with clean armored shell pattern, narrow waist line, folded shoulder armor, claw/finger articulation, and readable serpentine posture.

Signature palette: deep slate `#1f2430`, black-purple `#362a4b`, muted copper `#a16f3f`, steel gray `#8ea3a9`, ghost white `#d6d7de`, and soft gold `#bda65d`.

Lineage relationship: coordinates with Pucci through muted gold and slate while shifting the dominant body colors into darker shell tones.

Core ability cue: an attached gold-bright segment or line set on the arm or torso that remains part of the same body mass.

## C-MOON

Identity lock: compact angular Stand form, bent-forward kinetic posture, widened jaw plate, heavy shoulder armor, layered chest ridges, and a distinct altered balance and silhouette.

Signature palette: charcoal `#1e1f25`, moon-amber `#8f5b2d`, graphite blue `#233245`, pale lavender `#7d79a6`, silver `#d3d7de`, and bruised magenta `#5f3f6f`.

Lineage relationship: carries Pucci’s muted shell geometry cues while adding deeper violet-night and lunar contrast in C-MOON’s dominant forms.

Core ability cue: an attached crescent-angled shard strip fused to an arm or waist that reads as phase-shift pressure.

## Made in Heaven

Identity rank: apex antagonist form in the Part 6 arc, compact but fast and dense silhouette, stretched limbs, sharp shoulder/hip transitions, and one dominant temporal pulse band.

Signature palette: midnight blue `#13162b`, storm steel `#3c4a6e`, electric amber `#f0b85a`, pale mint `#5fb5a7`, off-white `#e8e4d8`, and violet flash `#7d79b8`.

Lineage relationship: the final form should still share Pucci’s shell logic and muted gold accents but with the highest contrast and the clearest temporal pulse accent.

Core ability cue: an attached amber-forward directional pulse fused to chest or fist, with short, repeated temporal bands that remain part of the same form and never detach.

## Batch acceptance

All six pets require complete 1536x2288 Codex Pet V2 atlases, three isolated blind direction reviews, explicit labeled direction semantics, continuity review, independent final visual QA, local installation verification, and one Part 6 QA overview highlighting every accepted warning before release.
