# Wave 2 Part 4 Visual Brief

Status: production brief for the first Wave 2 batch. These are project-authored free-color interpretations, not canonical JoJo palettes. All four pets passed engineering QA and owner review and were publicly released on 2026-07-21.

## Shared contract

- Unified chibi pixel system: compact three-head-tall full body, chunky pixel clusters, hard dark contours, limited palette, and a readable `192x208` silhouette.
- One locked signature palette per pet. Character and Stand pairs coordinate through selected accents without using identical palettes.
- State meanings remain fixed. Character-specific performance and ability cues may vary only when they reinforce the assigned Codex state.
- Ability cues must be opaque, hard-edged, and attached to or overlapping the pet. No detached shards, bombs, bubbles, sparks, symbols, shadows, scenery, text, or combined character-and-Stand pets.

## Josuke Higashikata

Identity lock: compact athletic teenager, tall sculpted pompadour joined cleanly to the head, expressive brows, high-collar school uniform, simplified heart and peace-shaped metal pins, broad trousers, and sturdy shoes.

Signature palette: near-black violet `#12122c`, midnight violet `#2b1b52`, petrol teal `#167f7c`, hot coral `#ef5d6d`, warm gold `#e7b84d`, and pale ice `#a8edf0`.

Core ability cue: an attached coral-and-gold faceted repair seam. It may reinforce focused task work, a failed fractured-to-repaired recovery, or completed review. Crazy Diamond never appears inside this pet.

## Crazy Diamond

Identity lock: powerful but compact armored humanoid Stand, heart-like forehead crest, rounded helmet and jaw planes, broad shoulder armor, segmented gauntlets, and large readable heart and diamond motifs.

Signature palette: dark plum `#21152d`, amethyst `#6b4bc3`, bright jade `#4ac7a6`, warm cream `#f5e4bd`, hot coral `#ef5d6d`, and pale gold `#edc86b`.

Pair relationship: shares Josuke's coral repair accent while shifting the dominant body colors to amethyst and jade.

Core ability cue: a hard-edged fractured facet that visibly rejoins while staying attached to a fist, forearm, or touched fragment. Use it in compatible task, failure-recovery, and review beats without loose debris.

## Yoshikage Kira

Identity lock: immaculate adult with carefully swept hair, calm narrow eyes, tailored suit, clean shirt and tie geometry, polished shoes, and controlled hand poses. The tie may use one simplified cat-skull-like motif but no text or repeated micro-detail.

Signature palette: ink plum `#17142b`, warm ivory `#f1e8cf`, pale mint `#b8e5d5`, raspberry `#c64e83`, antique gold `#e0ad47`, and cool cyan `#4aaec0`.

Core ability cue: a compact opaque raspberry-and-cyan charge fused to a fingertip, thumb, or palm. It may reinforce deliberate task work, tense waiting, or a contained failed reaction. Killer Queen never appears inside this pet.

## Killer Queen

Identity lock: compact feline humanoid Stand, pointed ear-like helmet, smooth mask, narrow cat eyes, armored torso and limbs, wrist spikes, and large simplified heart or cat motifs readable at pet size.

Signature palette: deep aubergine `#291636`, pale jade `#d8f0d3`, raspberry `#c64e83`, cool cyan `#4aaec0`, warm gold `#e0ad47`, and charcoal `#171820`.

Pair relationship: shares Kira's raspberry, cyan, and gold accents while using pale jade armor and a deeper aubergine silhouette.

Core ability cue: an opaque attached palm charge or ringed pressure pulse that remains connected to the hand or torso. Bites the Dust stays an ability mode expressed only as a restrained attached pulse in semantically compatible states; it is not a separate pet or detached icon.

## Batch acceptance

All four pets require complete 1536x2288 Codex Pet V2 atlases, three isolated blind direction reviews, explicit labeled direction semantics, continuity review, independent final visual QA, local installation verification, and one Part 4 QA overview highlighting any accepted warnings before release.
